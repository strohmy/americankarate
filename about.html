American Karate was established in Antioch, California in 1988 at the Raley's Shopping Center on Lone Tree Way. After 14 years at that location we moved to Brentwood, California to the Sand Creek Crossing Shopping Center and opened in November, 2002. In 2017 we moved to 8650 Brentwood Blvd. Suite H. The academy is one of the most respected in the Contra Costa County area.

Classes are taught in a relaxed atmosphere and are fun for the entire family. Karate has so much more to offer than just kicking or punching. We teach life skills. Our students develop coordination, confidence, goal setting, and leadership skills. It's a terrific way to release stress giving you more energy, getting you in great shape, and is fun to do.

Instructors

Don Rethage, Master Instructor

Don Rethage, owner of American Karate, started training in 1977 while in the United States Air Force at Dover A.F.B., Delaware. After spending 18 months at Kadena A.B. in Okinawa, he found that cross training in other styles makes for a well rounded student.

Mr. Rethage holds Black Belts in Isshinryu Karate, Alemany Kenpo, Kemscrima-Doh, and Doce Pares Eskrima. His current rank is 8th degree Black Belt, promoted by Grandmaster Rick Alemany. Mr. Rethage was inducted into the U.S.A. Martial Arts Hall of Fame in 2006 in the category of Mixed Martial Arts Master. Mr. Rethage has trained with some of the top instructors in their respected disciplines:

-Reese Rigby, 9th degree Isshinryu Karate
-Grandmaster Rick Alemany, 10th degree Kenpo
-Grandmaster Glenn Abrescy, Kemscrima-Doh
-Grandmaster Cacoy Canete, 12th degree Doce Pares Eskrima
-Grandmaster Carley Gracie, 9th degree Gracie Jiu-Jitsu
-Ricardo Kayanan, Sayoc Kali
-George Tsutsui, 4 Time Muay Thai World Champion

Suzanne Brown, 3rd Degree Black Belt

Suzanne offers intense, fun training and full body workouts. Suzanne is a certified ISSA Fitness Trainer, Specialist in Fitness Nutrition and CPR and EFA certified. She has been training in Martial Arts for 15 years and has a 3rd Degree Black Belt in Isshin Ryu Karate. She is also a certified Mixed Martial Arts Instructor/CMMI, Certified Martial Arts Instructor/CMI, Certified Karate Instructor/CKI. Suzanne works closely with each client to personalize workouts to fit their needs and goals.
